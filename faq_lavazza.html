CAN I PREPARE A COLD BREW WITH LAVAZZA GROUND COFFEE?

You are welcome to try different brewing methods, since coffee is completely subjective and perhaps find a recipe you will love. Lavazza ground coffee can be used to prepare your favorite cold brew recipes. Please see some recipes from Lavazza at XXURLXX.

IS LAVAZZA WHOLE BEAN COFFEE VACUUM-SEALED?

All bags of Lavazza whole bean coffee are fitted with a patented one-way valve allowing air and naturally occurring gases to exit the bag, while preventing oxygen from entering. This patented technology preserves freshness and the organoleptic properties of all Lavazza whole bean coffee.

IS LAVAZZA COFFEE KOSHER?

Most Lavazza coffees are Kosher and you are welcome to email us and request the Kosher certificates.

WHAT IS THE RECOMMENDED COFFEE RATIO FOR LAVAZZA COFFEE?

The coffee ratio is to use 1 scoop per 8oz cup.

MY BRICKS OF COFFEE ARE PUFFY, IS THAT AN ISSUE?

All bricks of Lavazza coffee, including Crema e Gusto and Qualita Rossa, are fully sealed when shipped by our warehouse. If you squeeze the brick and no air exits, the seal is fully intact and the brick of coffee fully sealed - there is no impact on freshness or preservation.

IS LAVAZZA PACKAGING RECYLCLABLE?

Yes, Lavazza packaging can be recylced if indicated on the product packaging.

ARE THEY ANY NUTS IN LAVAZZA COFFEES?

Lavazza products contain only one ingredient, coffee, and are produced in facilities that exclusively make coffee.

IS LAVAZZA COFFEE GLUTEN FREE?

There is no gluten in any of our ingredients, but we cannot guarantee gluten-free contact along the supply chain.

WHY IS THERE A HOLE UNDER THE LAVAZZA COFFEE TIN?

ARE LAVAZZA CAPSULES BPA AND OTHER PLASTICIZER FREE?

The materials in contact with the food products used by Lavazza are produced through processes which do not envisage the intentional use of BPA.
