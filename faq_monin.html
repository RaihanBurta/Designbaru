WHAT MONIN PRODUCTS ARE GLUTEN FREE?

Our Premium Syrups, including Sugar Free, Concentrates, Organics, Zero Calorie Natural Flavorings and Sweeteners as well as our Fruit Purées, Sauces and Fruit Smoothie are Gluten Free.

WHAT MONIN PRODUCTS ARE NON-GMO?

Our Premium Syrups, including Sugar Free, Concentrates, Organics, Zero Calorie Natural Flavorings and Sweeteners as well as our Fruit Purées, Sauces and Fruit Smoothie Mixes are non-GMO.

DO ANY MONIN FLAVORS CONTAIN CAFFEINE?

Monin Espresso Syrup , True Brewed Espresso Concentrate and Iced Coffee Concentrate contain real coffee bean extracts, so they do contain some caffeine. The amount is generally equal to or lower than one would expect to consume in an average size cup of regular coffee or espresso-based beverage. Amount of caffeine per 1 oz.

DO ANY MONIN FLAVORS CONTAIN NUT ALLERGENS?

Yes, the following Monin products contain nut allergens. Allergens are handled following strict FDA guidelines and internal HACCP (Hazard Analysis of Critical Control Points) program. Please contact us directly for any additional allergen-related questions.

DO MONIN FLAVORS CONTAIN ANY DAIRY INGREDIENTS?

Monin Syrups (including Premium , Organic and Sugar Free Syrups ), Sweeteners , Fruit Purées , Beverage Concentrates and Fruit Smoothie Mixes do not contain dairy ingredients. Monin Gourmet Sauces do contain dairy ingredients. Monin Dark Chocolate Sauce does not contain dairy ingredients, while the other Gourmet Sauces do. Natural Frappé Base do contain dairy.

HOW ARE MONIN SUGAR FREE FLAVORS SWEETENED?

Monin Sugar Free Syrups are sweetened with a proprietary blend of sucralose and erythritol (an all-natural sugar substitute derived from plant sources). This combination of sweeteners provides clean, true flavor without the metallic aftertaste sometimes noticeable with other sugar free products.

HOW OFTEN ARE THE LABELS AND PRODUCT INFORMATION UPDATED ON THE SITE?

Monin strives to ensure that the most up to date product information is available to you online. Please note that occasionally the information on received product may vary from what is shown on the website, due to manufacturing changes and quality assurance. Always check the physical label to verify nutritional information for the actual product you have received.

WHAT ARE MONIN'S QUALITY STANDARDS?

The goal at Monin Gourmet Flavourings is to produce the finest products available anywhere. In order to reach our goal, we implement rigorous procedures that ensure top quality at every stage of ingredient sourcing and production. Monin manufactures its products under a Hazard Analysis of Critical Control Points (HACCP) operating plan and Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) to minimize the risk of illness or injury. Monin Inc. is SQF Certified which is part of the Global Food Safety Initiative (GFSI) that was established to ensure confidence in the delivery of safer food to consumers, while continuing to improve food safety supply chain management. Under this global food safety standard, Monin’s worldwide commitment focuses on the safety and quality of its products, manufacturing, storage, and distribution. GFSI-benchmarked food safety standards include: Safe Quality Food (SQF) and British Retail Consortium (BRC). Additional auditing is conducted by the Kosher Supervision of America (KSA) and Islamic Food and Nutrition Council of America (IFANCA) to certify products Kosher and Halal. Quality Assurance International (Q.A.I.) certifies the plant for handling Organic products in accordance with the National Organic Program (NOP).

ARE MONIN PRODUCTS VEGAN?

At Monin, we pride ourselves on creating exceptional flavourings, sauces, purées, and fruit smoothie mixes. All of our products are made with the highest quality ingredients, and the majority are clean label, free from artificial flavours, colours, preservatives, and sweeteners. While we do not use animal-derived ingredients in most of our product categories (our sauces do contain dairy), some of the sugar used in our products is not vegan. Our long-term goal is to retain a sugar supplier whose processing meets vegan requirements. Until then, by the strictest definition, some of our products (including our premium syrups) are unsuitable for vegans.

ARE MONIN FLAVORS HALAL?

The majority of Monin flavours are Halal in accordance with the rules set forth by Ifanca (Halal-food certification organization). Please contact us for a complete listing.

ARE MONIN FLAVORS KOSHER?

Yes, all Monin flavors are Kosher.
