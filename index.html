Frequently Asked Questions

What does KMDS sell?

At KMDS, we offer a diverse range of high-quality products catering to the food and beverage industry including Monin, Lavazza, Milklab ,Bobaking, Santino, KAV, IZY, JTC OmniBlend, Strawme, and I Pilot.

How to order products from KMDS?

You can order products through E-Commerce (Tokopedia), contact the sales team, showroom, or send an email to info@kmds.co.id.

Are these products available for delivery throughout Indonesia?

Yes, we have retail and subdistributors who will deliver our products throughout Indonesia.

If there is damage to the product, what should I do?

If there is any damage to the product you receive, the first step you should take if you are buying online is to make sure to video record the unboxing process. Then, you must immediately file a complaint within 24 hours of receiving the product, after which we will identify the type of damage that occurred, ranging from quantity issues such as the number of items that do not match, to product issues such as products that are foamy, mixed up, or foreign objects found inside. If the product is shipped via our expedition service, please also report any damage or deterioration to the product. Please contact our customer service team with the necessary details and evidence for a proper solution.

What certifications does KMDS have?

KMDS has halal certification, BPOM certification, and ISO certification.
