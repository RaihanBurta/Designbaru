WHO IS SANTINO?

Santino is a dynamic and innovative company in the coffee and beverage industry, known for its commitment to delivering exceptional products and services. With a passion for quality and a customer-centric approach, Santino has become a trusted partner for businesses and enthusiasts seeking top-tier coffee solutions.

WHAT DOES SANTINO OFFER?

Santino offers a comprehensive range of products and services tailored for the coffee and beverage industry. This includes premium coffee beans, specialty blends, single-origin selections, powder drinks, beverage toppings, cutting-edge coffee machines, coffee grinders, flavored syrups, quality milk, and even ovens. Additionally, Santino provides services such as Barista Training, Menu Development with COGS, Personalized Business Solutions, and Extensive Support.

WHAT SETS SANTINO APART?

Santino stands out for its dedication to personalized solutions, ensuring that each client’s unique needs and aspirations are met. The company’s commitment to quality (Santino has several certifications, including ISO 22000, BPOM, and Halal), innovation, and comprehensive support distinguishes Santino as a reliable partner in the coffee and beverage business. The emphasis on community, sustainability, and a tailored approach sets Santino apart from the competition.

HOW CAN I ORDER SANTINO'S PRODUCTS?

Ordering Santino’s products is convenient and straightforward. Visit the Santino Coffee Official Store on Tokopedia or Shopee to explore the product catalog and place orders online. For personalized assistance or inquiries, you can contact Santino’s customer service team, who will guide you through the ordering process and address any specific requirements.

WHERE ARE SANTINO SHOWROOMS?

Santino’s showrooms are strategically located to provide accessibility for clients. You can find Santino Showrooms in key cities, including Jakarta, Tangerang, Surabaya, Yogyakarta, Banjarmasin, and Makassar. For the precise locations of Santino’s showrooms, refer to the official website or contact Santino’s customer service team.
